Dolfwick

Dolfwick was a film group that I was a part of for a high school film class. We didn't make good movies, but at least we had fun.

Our Films (In Order)

Citizen Kevin

This is a "silent" film since we weren't allowed to use microphones yet. The film is too long and the story is poorly explained. It has its moments but I wouldn't watch this one first.


Say No

Another psuedo silent film, Say No is a music video that tells the story of a teenager who gets picked on at school. This one is much shorter and simpler to follow. You might even enjoy yourself a little bit.


Minnesota Thomas

Our first film with dialogue, Minnesota Thomas is an Indiana Jones parody wherein a young student must secure an ancient Nazi treasure.


The Delivery

My personal favorite, The Delivery is a horror-comedy that follows a group of friends and their encounter with a dangerous pizza company.


The Rookie

They say Rome wasn't built in a day, but this film was. The Rookie is a COPS parody which follows a suspicious cop and his new partner as they try to take down a notorious "Rock, Paper, Scissors" champion.
